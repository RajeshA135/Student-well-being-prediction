About Student Wellbeing Prediction

The Student Wellbeing Prediction model revolutionizes traditional mental health assessments. By analyzing student stress and sleep quality—key factors impacting academic performance and health—this project employs advanced machine learning techniques. Utilizing Perceived Stress Scale (PSS) and Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index (PSQI) scores, our Gradient Boosting algorithm classifies student wellbeing into three categories: Good, Average, or Bad, enabling early intervention and support.

Project Features

Data-Driven Insights

Analyzes stress and sleep data from real-world student datasets for actionable insights.

Automated Prediction

Classifies student wellbeing into Good, Average, or Bad with high accuracy.

Early Risk Detection

Identifies students at potential risk early, facilitating timely support.

Machine Learning Based

Powered by the robust Gradient Boosting Algorithm for reliable predictions.

User Authentication

Secure registration and login for students to submit their details safely.

Admin Dataset Upload

Enables easy uploading of new datasets to retrain the model.

Responsive Design

Built with Bootstrap for a seamless experience across all devices.
